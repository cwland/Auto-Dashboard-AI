Welcome to Auto Dashboard AI

Turn your bookmarks into a beautiful, AI-enriched dashboard that appears every time you open a new tab. Setup takes about two minutes.

1

Get an OpenRouter API Key

This extension uses OpenRouter to power the AI enrichment — it gives you access to dozens of models from a single key. Free-tier credits are available and are more than enough to generate several dashboards.

Open OpenRouter →
💡 Create a free account, then click Create Key. Copy the key — you'll paste it in the next step.
2

Enter & Validate Your Key

Click the Settings tab above, paste your API key into the field, and click Validate. Once validation succeeds, click Save Settings.

🔒 Your API key is stored locally on your device only. It is never sent to any server other than OpenRouter.
3

Choose an AI Model

In Settings, pick a model from the dropdown. The default Gemini Flash 1.5 is fast, accurate, and inexpensive. If you'd rather pay nothing, choose one of the Free models.

⚡ Gemini Flash 1.5 typically processes 50 bookmarks in under 10 seconds.
4

Create Your First Dashboard

Open the Dashboards tab. Use the bookmark tree on the left to select folders or individual bookmarks. Give your dashboard a name, then click ✨ Generate Dashboard. The AI will add descriptions and resolve icons automatically.

🎉 After generating, open a new tab — your dashboard will be waiting for you!
📊 Your Dashboard
🗂️

Folder sections

Bookmarks are organized by the folder structure from your browser, making it easy to scan at a glance.

🖱️

Click to open

Click any bookmark card to open that URL in a new tab. Middle-click also works as expected.

💬

Hover for description

Hover over any bookmark to see the AI-generated description tooltip with context about that site.

🎨

Smart icons

Icons are pulled directly from each site (apple-touch-icon → favicon), then from Simple Icons, then Google — with an emoji as a last resort.

🔍 Search
⌨️

Instant filter

Type in the search bar to instantly filter visible bookmarks across all sections by name, URL, or description.

📂

Auto-hides empty sections

Folder sections with no matches are hidden automatically while you search, keeping results clean.

🗃️ Multiple Dashboards
➕

Create as many as you need

Make separate dashboards for Work, Personal, Research, or any theme. Each has its own bookmark set and AI metadata.

⭐

Set a default

Mark any dashboard as Default — that's the one that appears on new tabs and at browser startup.

🔀

Switch on the fly

A dropdown appears in the top-right of your dashboard whenever you have two or more dashboards saved.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts
Key Action
/ Focus the search bar

💡 Tips
🔄

Regenerate anytime

Delete a dashboard and regenerate it after adding new bookmarks. The AI will pick up any new entries.

🆓

Free models work great

Llama 3.1 8B and Mistral 7B (both free on OpenRouter) handle bookmark enrichment well for most users.

🔒

Privacy first

Only the URLs and titles of your selected bookmarks are sent to the AI. Nothing else ever leaves your device.
